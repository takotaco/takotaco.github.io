Tutorial

Welcome to the tutorial. Let's get started.

First let's name this chart.

Click on the pen icon next to 'Untitled' above this box.

Enter the name of your chosing and click 'update'. This will rename your chart.

Now let's make a box.

Click on the canvas to the right, an input box should appear next to where you clicked.

Type in the text you want to appear in the box, and press 'add'.

A box will appear on the canvas with that text.

Now make another box.

Again, click on the canvas, input text, and press 'add'

Another box will appear on the canvas.

Now let's move these boxes around.

To move a box, hover your mouse over the box. A anchor point should appear in the top left corner of the box.

Click on the anchor point, and drag the box to the desired location.

Now lets connect the boxes with an arrow!

To make an arrow, click on the first box and drag your mouse to the second box. An arrow should appear between them.

Now let's select one of the boxes to add more detailed information.

To selecte a box, simply double click on it.

The box should now be highlighted in orange.

Since the box is selected, in the panel below you can add details and references.

In 'Details' you can add more information about how to complete the step in this box.

In 'References' you can add links, seperated by a comma, to resources you used when researching this information.

Neither are required.

Either add details and references and hit 'save' in the box below, or click 'next'.

Great job!

That's it for the tutorial. If you have further questions or suggestions for improvement, hit the 'Feedback' button at the bottom of the page and send us an email!

Thanks and good luck!
